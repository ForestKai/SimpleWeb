Assignments

There will be three assignments for this course. Each assignment will be worth 10% of your final grade for a total of 30%. Assignments are done with partners.

Assignments are to be submitted on MarkUs as described in each assignment's individual handout. Improperly submitted assignments may not be graded. All assignments must be able to compile and execute on UTSC lab machines. All assignments must be submitted before 11:59 PM on their given due date. A silent policy is enacted 12 hours before this time, during which no questions about the assignment will be answered by any TA or course instructor.

Assignment 1

In assignment 1 you will be working with relational algebra and SQL. You will be asked to come up with expressions in relational algebra to obtain the desired information from a set of databases. You will also come up with SQL queries for similar purposes. Assignment 1 is due on February 9.

Assignment 2

In assignment 2 you will be working with HTML and CSS. You may use JavaScript but it is optional and not required. You will come up with a website for CSCB20 that describes everything in the course for future students. Assignment 2 is due on March 12.

Assignment 3

In assignment 3 you will be working with JavaScript. Assignment 3 is due on March 30.
