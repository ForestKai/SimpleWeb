Midterm

There will be one midterm. The midterm will be 110 minutes in length. The midterm will occur during lecture time (9-11 AM) on 2017, February 12. Room assignments are based on your last name and are as follows:

  • A-N in SW319
  • P-T in MW120
  • U-Z in PO101

Ensure you go to the correct room. Please have your student ID or some other government issued photo ID available when writing the midterm exam. The exam is closed book. It will cover everything from the start of the semester until part way through week 6. You will not be allowed to leave in the first and last 10 minutes of the midterm.
