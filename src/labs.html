Labs

Labs begin from the week of 15th January. We will try and post the lab handout for every week by 5pm Sunday. This will give you some time to go over the lab handout and prepare before the lab begins.

Labs are mandatory parts of the course. Your TA for your lab will be taking attendance. During labs you may be assigned a group to get some hands-on practice with the course material.

As a B-level computer science student you will have access to BV473. This is the lab where tutorials will be held. The availability of this lab and other computer labs on campus can be seen here.
